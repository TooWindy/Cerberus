A Short Guide on Cerberus

In this guide, I will briefly go over on how to kill Cerberus. Cerberus is a level 318 hellhound boss that is located in her lair, deep beneath the Taverley Dungeon in the cave entrance in the north-east part of the hellhound area which is found beyond the poisonous spiders. Cerberus requires a Slayer level of 91, along with a task of hellhounds or Cerberus herself.

Gear Recommendations

Melee

Melee appears to be the best in terms of dps. Here is the best in slot gear as well as what your inventory should look like:

  • Head: Slayer helm(i)
  • Neck: Amulet of Torture*
  • Chest: Bandos Chestplate**
  • Legs: Bandos Tassets**
  • Boots: Primordial Boots
  • Gloves: Ferocious Gloves
  • Cape: Inferno Cape
  • Weapon: Scythe of Vitur
  • Ammo Slot: Any blessing

    Inventory:

  • Ghrazi Rapier

  • Bandos Godsword****

  • Prayer Potions
